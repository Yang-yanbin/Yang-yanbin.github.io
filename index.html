Handstool

The lifting and supporting actions of the hands are transplanted into the design of the stool. After sitting down, the surface of the stool is not visible, as if there are many hands lifting in the air, and the center of gravity is unstable, which can be shaken or rotated at will. The stool consists of IKEA HANDSKALADS decorative wooden hands, pine wood tops, 23mm and 18mm thick wooden sticks and ABS connectors, all of which are made of wood except the connectors.

HUAKAI Brand Visual Identity System

Logo is a visual symbol of a brand, but also conveys the spirit and concept of the brand. Hukai Power's logo is taken from its initials: HK. Abstract graphics are designed by connecting "H" and "K". The small square on the left represents different devices, while the blue square linked in the middle represents connectivity and win-win cooperation. The right half of the shape is dynamic and has the potential of taking off.

Rebirth

The ring is broken and recombined to form three segments, which are overlapped layer by layer and linked together. The metallic luster is complemented by the earthiness of the coffee grounds. The coffee grounds themselves emit a subtle smell over time, which is enhanced by wear and tear. The spins tighten or unscrew, each time as if the gears of life were turning. The three bands combine the coffee grounds composite with different metals, and come in silver, dark gray and light rose gold.
